Cebu Eastern College: Enrollment Information

❗❗ ENROLLMENT SCHEDULE FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2025-2026 ❗❗

Early Enrollment Period: February 1-28, 2025
  • 10% discount on tuition fees
  • Priority section placement
Regular Enrollment Period: March 1 - May 31, 2025
  • Standard rates apply
  • Section placement based on availability
Late Enrollment Period: June 1-15, 2025
  • Subject to 5% late enrollment fee
  • Limited section options

You may also do walk-in enrollment.
Just go directly to the Basic/College Registrar Office and bring the following documents:

Requirements for New Students

  1. Report Card (Form 138)
  2. Copy of PSA Birth Certificate
  3. 2x2 ID picture with white background (4pc)
  4. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  5. 500.00 Enrollment fee for Non-Cebu City Scholar
  6. Certificate of Transfer/Honorable Dismissal (for transferees)

Requirements for Returning Students

  1. Clearance from previous school year
  2. Updated student information form
  3. Receipt of previous year's final payment

Curriculum Offerings

Elementary Department

Our elementary curriculum follows the enhanced K-12 program with special focus on:
  • Solid foundation in Mathematics and Science
  • English proficiency development
  • Filipino language and culture appreciation
  • Character development and values formation

Junior High School

The JHS curriculum emphasizes:
  • Advanced Mathematics and Science concepts
  • Research skills development
  • Technology and computer literacy
  • Comprehensive humanities program

Senior High School Tracks

Academic Track
  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics)
  • ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)
  • HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)
Technical-Vocational Track
  • Computer Programming
  • Electrical Installation and Maintenance
  • Hospitality Management
